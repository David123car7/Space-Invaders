# Space Invaders 

## Player

- Moves to the right or left
- Shoots 
- Has x amount of lives

## Enemies

There will be 2 different enemies, one white other red

- Both move to the right or left

### White Enemies

- Will have different sprites
- Will shoot at different speeds
- When killed will give different points
- It spawns at the start and after all white enemies are killed

The idea is to put the enemies line by line and the more far away the enemie is from the player, more speed the shoots will have and more points the enemie will give after killed

### Red Enemies

- Spawns at the start and moves fast, after appearing to the player it must take x seconds to appear again
- After killed it has to wait x seconds to appear again
- This enemie will be the one to give more points
